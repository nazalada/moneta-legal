Introduction

Moneta ("we", "our", or "the app") is a personal finance tracking application. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

Moneta stores all your financial data locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework. We do NOT:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics services
  • Show advertisements

What Data is Stored Locally

  • Transactions (income/expenses)
  • Account information
  • Budget settings
  • Categories
  • App preferences

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, managed entirely by Apple. We have no access to this data.
